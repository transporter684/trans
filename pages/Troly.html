...Mahindra DI 3200 Jayo Truck...

Mahindra DI 3200 Jayo is a truck with gross vehicle weight of 4990 kg. The engine powering this Mahindra truck is 2500 cc mDI Tech, FuelSmart Technology, producing 80 HP of power and 220 Nm of maximum torque. The engine of Mahindra DI 3200 Jayo truck is coupled with a 5 speed (5F+1R) gearbox offering superior mileage. This Mahindra DI 3200 Jayo truck comes in 4x2 axle configuration. The wheelbase of 2654 mm, allows this Mahindra truck to offer load body length suitable for different kinds of applications. Hydraulic Brakes offer superior safety in Mahindra DI 3200 Jayo and semi elliptical leaf springs suspension in front offers good ride quality.
